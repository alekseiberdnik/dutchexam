About DutchExam.online

DutchExam.online is a small, focused project with a simple goal: make Dutch exam preparation reliable, affordable and always available.

We are building tools that mirror the structure of the official inburgering exam, starting with Reading (Lezen) and Knowledge of Dutch Society (KNM).

Our approach

Instead of yet another generic language app, DutchExam.online is designed specifically for people preparing for the Dutch integration exams at level A2–B1.

  • Exam-style questions based on realistic Dutch letters, e-mails and situations.
  • Timed sessions that match official exam timing.
  • A clean dashboard where you can see your attempts and progress.

Research & quality

We follow best practices from large-scale language assessment:

  • Questions are tagged by skill (Reading, KNM), difficulty and topic.
  • We collect anonymous statistics to see which questions are too easy or too hard.
  • Over time we adjust and expand the question bank to stay aligned with the current inburgering exam format.

Roadmap

During the MVP phase, all tests are free. Our roadmap includes:

  • Listening, speaking and writing modules.
  • AI feedback on writing tasks (Schrijven).
  • Certificates and detailed progress reports for subscribers.
