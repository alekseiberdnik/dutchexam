How we determine your level

Adaptive

We start with simpler questions and gradually move to harder ones. The test adapts to your answers in real time: correct responses unlock more challenging tasks, while mistakes trigger targeted checks from another angle.

Accurate

The more tasks you complete, the more precisely we estimate your level. We triangulate your accuracy, speed, and consistency to avoid over‑ or under‑estimation. Patterns matter more than one‑offs.

Transparent

After the test you’ll see what influenced the result: strengths, typical mistakes, and next steps. Re‑take specific skills (e.g., Luisteren) to improve the overall estimate.
