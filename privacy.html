Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

What data we collect

  • Account data: email address and basic profile information.
  • Test data: which tests you start, your answers and scores.
  • Technical data: browser type, approximate location, pages viewed (via Google Analytics if you accept cookies).

Why we collect this data

  • To provide and improve the practice tests.
  • To understand which questions are too easy or too hard.
  • To keep your results linked to your account.

Cookies and analytics

We use a cookie banner to ask for your consent before enabling Google Analytics. You can change your browser settings to remove cookies at any time.

Data storage

Data is stored with reputable cloud providers (Supabase, Netlify and related services). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or want your data removed, please contact us using the details on the site once they are available.
